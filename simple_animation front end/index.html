Maneskin

Euro vision winners

Italian rock music band

Måneskin is an Italian rock band that has taken the world by storm with their unique blend of alternative, indie, and psychedelic sounds. Formed in 2016, the band consists of Damiano David (vocals), Victoria De Angelis (bass), Thomas Raggi (guitar), and Ethan Torchio (drums). Måneskin gained widespread recognition after winning the Eurovision Song Contest 2021 with their song "Zitti e buoni", which showcased their high-energy performance and catchy hooks. Since then, they have released several successful singles and albums, including "Teatro d'ira: Vol. I", and have been praised for their raw energy, captivating live performances, and Damiano's distinctive vocals. With their genre-bending sound and charismatic stage presence, Måneskin is undoubtedly one of the most exciting and promising rock bands to emerge in recent years.
